Timestamp Microservice

A simple service that converts a date to Unix timestamp.

To use

Enter a "/" and a Unix timestamp or a date after the web address in the address bar and press enter.

You will then be taken to a page that shows the conversion in JSON format.


Examples

http://name.com/1441252800 will give you

unix: 1441252800 natural: "September 5, 2015"


http://name.com/September 5, 2015 will give you

unix: 1441252800 natural: "September 5, 2015"
